About Us

ABN Group is a leader in all supply chain activities involving inbound logistics, storage & operations, outbound logistics, marketing & Sales, retailing and all related services of health Care products in Kerala. Pharmaceutical Products Portfolio includes ethical brands, branded generics, generics and OTC products of all therapeutic classes of almost all multinational and Indian national pharmaceutical companies. The group also deals with many leading products in food & nutrition, personal care, and life style products. Strong presence in the market covering public institutions, private hospitals & clinics and retail pharmacies located not only in cities but also in peripheral and remote locations. The group commenced operations in 2006 by acquiring the Flagship firm Varghese & Company, followed by acquisitions of ABN and Bruklyn in 2007 and 2011 respectively. The group has grown 100 times in sales and 5 times in number of employees since inception, in all these years has been consistently growing faster than the market. The operations is managed by partners with extensive understanding of the products and market of about two decades. Strategic and Finance management is by another partner with national & international professional certifications and exposure in diversified sectors including pharmaceutical in India and abroad.


Mission & Values:

Our mission is to be a leader in South India in providing affordable and superior health care, nutrition, personal care and life style products meeting the existing and future needs of community. Our Value system is anchored on non-compromising ethical business practices, superior quality, utmost professionalism and trust.
